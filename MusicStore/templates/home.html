Welcome to our music store management project!

We are a team consisting of Leyla and Jonnathan on the backend / Abraham and Rene on the frontend, and we are excited to introduce you to our project.

In our platform, we have developed a robust backend that offers a variety of endpoints through our API. Access to the information in our album collection does not require authentication. Here, you can find an overview of the available albums.

However, for more advanced functions such as creating, modifying, and deleting entries, as well as accessing the full view of the collection, authentication is necessary. We have implemented authentication tokens to ensure the security and privacy of our users.

Additionally, our API integrates with the Deezer API, allowing us to complement our collection with additional information about the albums, such as covers, artists, and more.

In the frontend, we have used Django and Bootstrap to create an intuitive and appealing user experience. Here, we combine the information stored in our API with data provided by Deezer API, providing a complete experience for music lovers.

Thank you for visiting our project, and we hope you enjoy exploring our music album collection!
